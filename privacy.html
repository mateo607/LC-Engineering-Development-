Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

1. Introduction

LC Engineering & Development LLC ("LC Engineering," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of individuals who interact with our website located at lcengineeringdevelopment.com (the "Site") and submit information through our contact and parcel submission forms. LC Engineering is a Florida limited liability company that operates across Florida and other states in the United States.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and your rights regarding that information. By using our Site or submitting a form, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

When you submit a parcel inquiry or contact form on our Site, we may collect the following information:

  • Full name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Parcel address or location
  • Approximate lot size
  • Zoning or intended use
  • Your role (landowner, investor, builder, etc.)
  • Any additional notes or details you voluntarily provide

We do not collect payment information, government identification, or sensitive personal data through our Site.

3. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To evaluate whether a submitted parcel meets our internal land acquisition criteria
  • To contact you regarding your submission via phone, email, or text message
  • To follow up on land acquisition inquiries and opportunities
  • To send transactional communications related to your submission
  • To send marketing communications if you have provided explicit consent

We do not use your information for any purpose unrelated to land acquisition and the operation of LC Engineering & Development LLC.

4. SMS and Text Message Communications

By providing your phone number and checking the consent box on our form, you agree to receive text messages from LC Engineering & Development at the number provided. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For assistance, reply HELP. Message and data rates may apply. Frequency varies.

We use SMS communications to follow up on parcel submissions, confirm receipt of inquiries, and share relevant acquisition opportunities when you have consented to marketing messages. SMS consent data is stored securely and is never sold or shared with third parties for their own marketing purposes.

5. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Your information is stored securely within GoHighLevel (GHL), a third-party customer relationship management (CRM) platform used to manage contacts and communications. GoHighLevel maintains its own privacy and security standards. We do not store personal data on our own servers.

We take reasonable measures to protect the information we collect from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers (such as GoHighLevel) who assist in operating our business and who are bound by confidentiality obligations
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of LC Engineering & Development LLC or others

SMS opt-in data and consent records are never shared with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our Site may use basic cookies or browser storage to improve your experience. We do not currently use third-party advertising trackers or retargeting pixels. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information from our records
  • Opt out of SMS or email communications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@lcend.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information through our Site, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the Site after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
